Our Story

A Stay Beyond Expectations

NS Comforts is Nanjangud's finest modern lodge, ideally situated near the sacred Srikanteshwara Temple — just 1.5 km away on Ooty Road, Nanjangud. A newly built property, meticulously maintained to offer guests a clean, comfortable, and welcoming experience.

Whether you're here for a pilgrimage, a family holiday, or a business trip, we ensure every need is met with warmth and promptness.
